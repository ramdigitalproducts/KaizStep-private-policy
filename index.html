Privacy Policy for KaizStep

Effective Date: May 19, 2026

Welcome to KaizStep. KaizStep is a walking and fitness tracking application designed to help users improve their health through walking routines, step tracking, and wellness statistics.

This Privacy Policy explains how KaizStep collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Health & Activity Data

  • Step count
  • Walking distance
  • Calories burned
  • Active minutes
  • Workout history
  • Walking statistics

Device Information

  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • App version
  • Anonymous diagnostic and crash data

Optional Information

  • Weight
  • Height
  • Fitness goals
  • Account information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Track walking activity
  • Display fitness statistics
  • Improve app performance
  • Save workout progress
  • Provide health insights
  • Improve user experience

3. Data Storage

KaizStep currently stores most data locally on your device.

4. Health Data Permissions

KaizStep may request access to motion and fitness activity, pedometer data, and future integrations such as Apple Health or Google Fit.

KaizStep does not sell your health data.

5. Third-Party Services

KaizStep may use trusted third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage, and notifications.

6. Advertising

KaizStep currently does not display third-party advertisements.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

8. Children's Privacy

KaizStep is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

9. Your Rights

You may request deletion or correction of your data at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
